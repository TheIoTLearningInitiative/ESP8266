# Microcontroller

## ESP8266

The ESP8266 is a low-cost Wi-Fi chip with full TCP/IP stack and microcontroller capability produced by Shanghai-based Chinese manufacturer, Espressif. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/ESP8266)
